Diagnosis

Mechanics diagnose your car so they can figure out what the problem with your car is.
They use a diagnostic computer that gives codes that the mechanic can use to find the exact problem with car.
The mechanic can only do this when they see lights such as the check engine light or the ABS light.
Otherwise mechancs will have to try and find the problem with their senses.
